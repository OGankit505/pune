Traditional Music of Pune

Bhavageet

Bhavageet is a form of Marathi light music that emphasizes poetry and lyrics. It combines classical ragas with emotionally rich lyrics, making it a popular choice for expressing deep sentiments.

Lavani

Lavani is a traditional form of folk music and dance that originated in Maharashtra. It is known for its energetic beats, expressive dance movements, and bold lyrics. Lavani often addresses various social and cultural issues.

Natya Sangeet

Natya Sangeet is a unique form of musical drama that blends classical music with theatrical performance. It is characterized by its ability to convey complex emotions through both music and acting.

Dholki Music

Dholki music involves the use of the dholki, a percussion instrument, to create rhythmic beats. It is often accompanied by folk songs and is a popular choice for celebratory events and festivals.

Gondhal

Gondhal is a traditional folk art form and a ritualistic performance that originated in Maharashtra, India. It is commonly practiced in various regions of the state, including Pune. Gondhal is often performed during religious and social occasions, particularly to invoke the blessings of the deity.
