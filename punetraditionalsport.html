Traditional Sports of Pune

Kabaddi

Kabaddi is a popular traditional sport in Pune. It is a team sport that involves physical strength, strategy, and quick reflexes.

Wrestling

Wrestling has a rich tradition in Pune. The city has produced many accomplished wrestlers who have represented India on national and international platforms.

Kho-Kho

Kho-Kho is a tag sport that originated in Maharashtra. It is played by two teams, and the objective is to tag the opponents as quickly as possible.

Gilli Danda

Gilli Danda is a traditional stick and ball game that requires precision and skill. It is played in various parts of Pune during festivals and leisure time.

Langdi

Langdi is a popular rural sport in Pune. It involves hopping and balancing on one foot while trying to tag opponents. It requires agility and endurance.
