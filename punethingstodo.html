Things to Do in Pune

1. Explore Shaniwar Wada

Visit the historic Shaniwar Wada, the palace fort of the Peshwas, known for its architectural brilliance and rich history.

2. Visit Aga Khan Palace

Explore Aga Khan Palace, a historical landmark associated with Mahatma Gandhi's internment during the freedom struggle.

3. Enjoy Nature at Sinhagad Fort

Trek to Sinhagad Fort and enjoy panoramic views of the Sahyadri mountains and explore its historic significance.

4. Experience Pune's Culture at Raja Dinkar Kelkar Museum

Visit the museum to view an extensive collection of artifacts showcasing the cultural heritage of Maharashtra.

5. Shop at Pune's Local Markets

Explore markets like Laxmi Road and FC Road for shopping traditional clothing, jewelry, and souvenirs.

6. Indulge in Street Food Delights

Savor local delicacies like Vada Pav, Bhel Puri, and Misal Pav at popular street food stalls.

7. Attend Cultural Performances

Experience Pune's rich cultural heritage through performances like Lavani dance and classical music concerts.
