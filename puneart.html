Traditional Arts and Souvenirs of Pune

Paithani Sarees

Paithani sarees are renowned for their vibrant colors and intricate zari work. These handwoven silk sarees often feature peacock and flower motifs, making them a popular choice among women for special occasions.

Warli Paintings

Warli paintings are a form of tribal art originating from the Warli tribe in Maharashtra. These paintings use simple geometric shapes and lines to depict scenes of rural life, nature, and folklore. They make for unique and culturally rich souvenirs.

Bidriware

Bidriware is a form of metalwork that originated in Bidar, Karnataka but is popular in Pune as well. It involves the use of zinc, copper, and silver to create intricate designs on objects like vases, trays, and boxes. The black and silver contrast is characteristic of Bidriware.

Kolhapuri Chappals

Kolhapuri chappals are handcrafted leather sandals originating from the Kolhapur region of Maharashtra. They are known for their durability and unique style. These chappals are a popular souvenir choice for both men and women.

Copper Crafting

One of the famous art of Pune is copper crafting, the art of beaten copper work. The workers who work here are called Twashta Kasars who works mostly with copper and some brass, locally called tambats.
