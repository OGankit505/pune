Traditional and Popular Delicacies of Pune

Misal Pav

Misal Pav is a spicy curry made of sprouted moth beans, served with pav (bread). It's a staple breakfast in Pune.

Puran Poli

Puran Poli is a sweet flatbread filled with a mixture of chana dal, jaggery, and cardamom. It's a must-have during festivals.

Bakarwadi

Bakarwadi is a crunchy snack made of gram flour, spices, and coconut, rolled into spirals and fried until golden brown.

Sabudana Khichdi

Sabudana Khichdi is a fasting dish made with tapioca pearls, peanuts, potatoes, and flavored with spices.

Bhakri with Pitla

Bhakri is a thick, unleavened flatbread made from jowar (sorghum) flour, usually served with spicy gram flour curry called Pitla.
