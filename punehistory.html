Pune - Historical Overview

Pune is the 9th most populous city in India and one of the largest in the state of Maharashtra. The city first gained importance as the capital of the Bhonsle Marathas in the 17th century. It was temporarily captured by the Mughals but again served as the official Maratha Capital from 1714 until its fall to British in 1817. It was the seasonal capital of Bombay Presidency and is now a popular tourist resort, historic and religious monuments and cultural attractions.
